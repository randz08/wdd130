About Us

What is Road "BAYANIHAN" Help?

Road "BAYANIHAN" Help is an online platform for a 24 Hour Emergency for cars and motorcycles. It has also financial products such as car insurance, credit cards, and loans. Our goal is to drive financial inclusion in the Philippines by helping Filipinos compare and choose products that best suit their needs. We also focus on driving financial literacy through our blog and social media channels.


Established in 2020 as a solution for any problems encountered along the road.


At present, Road "BAYANIHAN" Help is home to 100+ employees. In 2021, we welcomed one of our biggest investors, Experian, who led the series B1 funding of $20 million to help deliver the Group’s mission in empowering people to build healthier financial lives.
